In a nutshell - this is what the app is about:

Symbee eliminates the need for you to ever send a file to anybody, ever upload the file to any server or website, ever email or ftp the file. Just share the files and let your buddies pick up the files they like and when they like. You do the same. Don't ask your buddies to send files, just browse and search the shared folders and pick up the files you like. Browse through the photos shared by your buddies as if they were on your machine, preview each photo and choose what to save and what to skip.

Some of the better features of the app are:

  • Share Folders [Screen Shot]
    Share your folders with your Google Talk IM Buddies. Login with your Google Talk account and start sharing folders with you Google Talk buddies. Once you share folders, you buddies will be able to instantly browse through your shared folders.
     
  • Browse Shared folders [Screen Shot]
    You can instantly browse folders that your buddies have shared
     
  • Search [Screen Shot]
    Search the folders that you buddies have shared. If your buddies are using Google desktop, Symbee uses Google desktop to provide the search results
     
  • Photo Preview and Sharing [Screen Shot]
    View image previews before you download them
     
  • Download Directories/Multiple Files [Screen Shot]
    You can select multiple files or whole directories to be copied
